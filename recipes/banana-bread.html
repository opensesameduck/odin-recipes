Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Description

This is a delicious and fluffy banana bread recipe using very easy ingredients. The brown butter and chocolate chip addition makes this a sweet treat and you'll never go back to your regular banana bread recipe again!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 mashed overripe bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 2-4 tbsp of milk (add only if the batter is too thick)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  1. Preheat the Oven:Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and adjust the rack to the center position. Preheating ensures that the oven is at the right temperature for baking, which helps the bread rise properly and cook evenly.
  2. Brown the Butter:In a medium saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium-low heat. As the butter melts, it will begin to foam. Swirl the pan occasionally to ensure even heating. The butter will then start to turn a golden brown color and develop a nutty aroma. This process usually takes about 5-8 minutes. Be careful not to burn the butter; once it reaches a rich brown color, remove it from the heat and transfer it to a heatproof bowl to cool.
  3. Prepare the Pan:Grease a 9 by 5-inch loaf pan or line it with parchment paper. This step ensures that the banana bread doesn’t stick to the pan and makes it easier to remove after baking.
  4. Mash the Bananas:In a large bowl, mash the overripe bananas with a fork until smooth. The riper the bananas, the sweeter and more flavorful your bread will be. Overripe bananas are also easier to mash and incorporate into the batter.
  5. Combine Wet Ingredients:To the mashed bananas, add the cooled brown butter, brown sugar, egg, and vanilla extract. Whisk everything together until well combined. The brown butter adds a rich, nutty flavor to the bread, while the brown sugar adds sweetness and moisture.
  6. Mix Dry Ingredients:In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Mixing the dry ingredients separately helps to ensure that they are evenly distributed throughout the batter, resulting in a uniform texture.
  7. Fold Wet And Dry Ingredients:Pour the dry ingredients into the bowl with the wet ingredients. Using a rubber spatula, gently fold the ingredients together until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to a dense and tough banana bread.TIP - If your batter is too thick, add in 2-4 tbsp of milk to thin it out.
  8. Add Chocolate Chips:Gently fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the batter. The chocolate chips add pockets of melted chocolate and extra sweetness to the bread.
  9. Transfer Batter to Pan:Pour the batter into the prepared 9 by 5 pan. Spread it out evenly with a spatula. Top with additional chocolate chips.
  10. Bake:Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 55-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. If the bread starts to brown too much on top, you can tent it with aluminum foil halfway through baking.
  11. Cool and Serve:Remove the brown butter chocolate chip banana bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing. This allows the bread to set and makes it easier to slice without crumbling.
  12. Enjoy!Once the brown butter chocolate chip banana bread has cooled, slice it into thick slices and serve. This rich & decadent banana bread makes it a delicious treat for any time of day. Serve it plain or with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of ice cream for an extra indulgent treat
